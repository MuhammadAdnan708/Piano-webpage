Upright Piano

88 Keys Home Use Compact Size

The upright piano is the most common type in modern households and teaching environments. With its vertical string design, it takes up less space and is suitable for families and schools. Although its tone is slightly less rich than a grand piano, it is more affordable, making it an ideal choice for beginners and practice.

¥50,000 - ¥2,000,000+

Grand Piano

88 Keys Professional Large Size

The grand piano is the pinnacle of piano design, with horizontal strings, longer length, and a larger soundboard, producing richer and fuller tones. It is mainly used in concert halls, performances, and recording studios, making it the first choice for professional pianists.

¥1,000 - ¥50,000

Digital Piano

88 Keys Electronic Sound Adjustable Volume

Digital pianos use electronic technology to simulate the sound and feel of traditional pianos. With adjustable volume and headphone output, they are perfect for night practice. Modern high-end models closely replicate the acoustic feel, while being low-maintenance and portable, making them ideal for city living.
